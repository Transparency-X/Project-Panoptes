# Case Studies

Below are summaries of groups currently in the dataset. For full structured records, see `data/groups/`.

- **NXIVM** – Sex trafficking cult that used blackmail collateral.
- **Twin Flames Universe** – Digital coercion via constant surveillance apps.
- **Shincheonji** – Biometric check-ins and mandatory attendance tracking.
- **Scientology** – Institutional espionage via the Office of Special Affairs.
- **People’s Temple** – Extreme physical and peer surveillance in Jonestown.
- **Jehovah’s Witnesses** – Peer-to-peer monitoring and shunning.
- **Aum Shinrikyo** – Intensive group surveillance and paranoia in a doomsday cult.
- **Rajneeshpuram** – Bio‑terror and wiretapping in an Oregon commune.
- **Teal Swan** – Online‑driven psychological coercion and digital surveillance of followers.

Detailed long‑form case studies (optional) will be added under `data/case-studies/`.
