# Safety & Ethical Disclaimer

**Researching high‑control groups, cults, and their surveillance techniques can expose you to legal threats, online harassment, and psychological distress.**  
Please read this entire disclaimer before contributing or using any materials from Project Panoptes.

## 1. Personal Safety

- **Do not attempt to infiltrate or directly contact active groups** for the purpose of gathering information. Such actions can be dangerous and may compromise both you and the project.
- **Avoid sharing personally identifying details** about yourself in contributions. Use pseudonyms if necessary.
- **Be aware that high‑control groups monitor their critics.** Even passive research may attract unwanted attention.

## 2. Data Privacy & Survivor Protection

- Never include private, identifying information about survivors without their explicit, informed consent.
- All data in this repository should be drawn from **publicly available sources** (news articles, court records, academic publications, authorised survivor testimonies).
- If you are a survivor and wish to contribute your story, please reach out through a secure channel (to be provided). We treat survivor contributions with extreme care and anonymity.

## 3. Mental Health Resources

Researching topics of abuse, manipulation, and cult dynamics can be emotionally taxing. If you feel overwhelmed, please consider reaching out to a professional.

**International support resources:**
- **Cult Information Centre (CIC)** – general information and support.
- **International Cultic Studies Association (ICSA)** – academic resources and referral.
- **Mind** (UK) / **NAMI** (USA) – general mental health support.

## 4. Legal

The maintainers of Project Panoptes provide this repository for educational and research purposes only. We make no representations as to the accuracy or completeness of any data. Use the information at your own risk.

By contributing, you agree that you have read and understood this disclaimer.
